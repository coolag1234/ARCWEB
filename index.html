Inside Of A Flash Drive

I just found a flash drive that I had previously disassembled and decided I wanted to know how it worked so I looked on the internet and this is what I learned...


  • Flash drives normally have a USB plug in but not always!
  • They use a USB mass storage controller, a small microcontroller with a small amount of on-chip ROM and RAM.
  • NAND flash memory chip(s) – stores data (NAND flash is typically also used in digital cameras).
